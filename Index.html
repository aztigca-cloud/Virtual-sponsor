Just for Today

NA Daily Meditation - Thursday, June 18, 2026

Keep It Simple

"We try to follow the dictates of a Power greater than ourselves." - Basic Text, p. 9

In recovery we learn that simplicity is the key to spiritual growth. When we complicate things, we create opportunities for our disease to take hold. Keeping it simple means following spiritual principles in all our affairs. We don't have to figure everything out today.

When we feel overwhelmed, we can ask: What is the next right thing? Usually the answer is simple - go to a meeting, call our sponsor, pray, help someone else. The program gives us simple tools that work if we work them.

Just for Today: I will keep it simple. I will practice honesty, open-mindedness, and willingness in all my affairs.

5-Minute Craving Plan

  1. 1.
    Pause & Breathe: Take 5 deep breaths. Cravings pass. You don't have to act on them.
  2. 2.
    Call Someone: Reach out to your sponsor, a friend, or a helpline. Don't use alone.
  3. 3.
    Change Your Space: Leave the room. Go outside. Get around other people.
  4. 4.
    H.A.L.T Check: Are you Hungry, Angry, Lonely, or Tired? Meet that need first.
  5. 5.
    Play the Tape: Remember where using leads. It's never just one.

Grounding Technique: 5-4-3-2-1

Use your senses to get back in your body and out of your head:

  • 5 things you can see
  • 4 things you can touch
  • 3 things you can hear
  • 2 things you can smell
  • 1 thing you can taste

Install Virtual Sponsor

Add this app to your phone's home screen for quick access, offline support, and a full-screen experience.

iPhone / iOS Android

Install on iPhone or iPad

  1. 1.
    Open this page in Safari. Other browsers won't work.
  2. 2.
    Tap the Share button at the bottom of the screen.
  3. 3.
    Scroll down and tap Add to Home Screen.
  4. 4.
    Name it "Virtual Sponsor" and tap Add.
  5. 5.
    The app icon will appear on your home screen for one-tap access.

Install on Android

  1. 1.
    Open this page in Chrome.
  2. 2.
    Tap the three dots menu in the top right.
  3. 3.
    Tap Install app or Add to Home Screen.
  4. 4.
    Confirm by tapping Install.
  5. 5.
    The app will be added to your home screen and app drawer.

Why Install?

  • Works offline - access Emergency and Just for Today with no internet
  • Full-screen app experience with no browser bars
  • Faster loading and one-tap access when you need it most
  • Feels like a native app, built for privacy
